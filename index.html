Features
Scheduly is a mobile app that talks to and schedules your employees so that you don’t have to.
Add Employees

Easily add all your employees’ contact information to the app.

Create a Shift

You can easily create a shift by choosing a date, time and number of employees needed.

Fill a Shift

Scheduly will magically talk to your employees and schedule them for the shift you created .

Scheduling Automated

Let Scheduly send out a text message to your employees notifying them that a shift is available. Employees can take a shift by simply replying with the code specified in the text message. Once all the shift slots have been filled, Scheduly will notify remaining employees of this - no more confusion and no more misunderstandings

Scheduly is automated and does everything in the background - adding employees to the schedule and more without you ever noticing. You can see who is taking the shift with ease.
